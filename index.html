About DaanKart

DaanKart is a platform that bridges the gap between donors and receivers. By making it simple to donate essentials, we aim to reduce waste and bring smiles to those in need.

How It Works

Donate

Donors list items like clothes, books.

Request

Receivers browse and request items they need.

Verify

Admin verifies and matches donations with requests.

Deliver

Items are delivered, ensuring smiles and impact.

Together, We Make a Difference

Every book shared, every piece of clothing donated, and every small act of kindness adds up to create a big change. Be part of this mission today.
